# East Tennessee two-site farm business plan — phase timeline

**Narrative detail** (deliverables, gates): [`10-year roadmap`](../analyses/east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-10-year-roadmap.md). **Package hub**: [`East Tennessee two-site farm business plan (package)`](../business-plan/east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan.md).

Calendar dates **slip**; **gates** in the validation backlog are authoritative for “go / no-go,” not the year labels below.

---

## Phase bands (planning)

| Phase | Name | Rough calendar band | Intent |
|------:|------|----------------------|--------|
| **0** | Immediate / no-regret groundwork | ~0–6 months | Stop bleeding; clarify use; cheap wins only |
| **1** | Low-cost proof + land intelligence | ~6 months–2 years | Carrying capacity, water, markets; telemetry pilot |
| **2** | First real operating business | ~Years 2–4 | Owned grazing or contract stack; positive operating cadence |
| **3** | Scale-up + optimization | ~Years 4–7 | Margin proof; automation OPEX review; optional second stream |
| **4** | Salary-replacement architecture | ~Years 7–10 | Farm cash approaches household need; resilience package |
