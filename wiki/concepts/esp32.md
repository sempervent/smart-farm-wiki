# ESP32

**ESP32** refers to Espressif’s family of low-cost SoCs widely used in **IoT**, hobby, and embedded products: integrated Wi‑Fi, many models with Bluetooth, multiple core options (Xtensa and later RISC‑V lines such as **C**/**S**/**H**/**P** series variants in vendor messaging).

In this wiki, ESP32 shows up as a practical MCU platform for **sensors**, **displays**, **ESPHome**, and **smart-farming** firmware experiments—distinct from RF-only stories like [`LoRa (radio)`](lora-radio.md) or [`Meshtastic`](meshtastic.md), though real builds may combine them.
