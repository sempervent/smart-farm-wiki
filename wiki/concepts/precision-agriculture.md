# Precision agriculture

**Precision agriculture** is a management approach that combines spatial/temporal data (soils, yields, imagery, machinery telemetry) with decision support to tune inputs and operations field-by-field or plant-by-plant—often associated with GPS/GNSS, variable-rate equipment, and increasingly with in-field wireless sensors.
