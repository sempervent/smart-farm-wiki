# Wi-Fi HaLow

**Wi-Fi HaLow** is the marketing name for **IEEE 802.11ah**, a sub-GHz amendment to the Wi‑Fi family aimed at longer range and lower power than 2.4/5/6 GHz Wi‑Fi—often discussed alongside **LoRa**/**LoRaWAN** for IoT backhaul and sensor densification.

Use this wiki’s source-notes for provenance; treat vendor and forum figures (range, throughput, power) as **claims** to validate for a specific band, hardware, and regulatory domain.
