# Decentralized physical infrastructure networks (DePIN)

**DePIN** (decentralized physical infrastructure network) names an architecture where participants contribute **physical** or **digital** infrastructure (sensors, bandwidth, storage, wireless coverage, and similar) and may receive tokenized incentives—often discussed adjacent to LPWAN technologies in crowd-deployed networks.

Definitions and market statistics vary by source; prefer primary documentation for any investment or deployment decision.
