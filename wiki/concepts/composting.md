# Composting

**Composting** is the **aerobic**, **managed** decomposition of **organic matter** (kitchen scraps, yard waste, manures, paper) into **compost**—a stable **soil amendment** rich in organic matter and plant nutrients, used to improve tilth, water retention, and biological activity in gardens, orchards, and fields.
