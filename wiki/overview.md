# Overview

This vault is a **structured synthesis layer** on top of immutable **raw** sources. The agent’s job is to compound knowledge: entities, concepts, timelines, and analyses linked with stable paths and explicit provenance.

**How to read this wiki**

1. Start at [`index.md`](index.md) for the catalog.
2. Follow [`log.md`](log.md) for chronological work.
3. Use **source-notes** under `source-notes/` to ground claims in `raw/processed/`.

**Principles**

- Raw sources are evidence; the wiki is the maintained model.
- Prefer incremental edits and explicit deprecation over silent rewrites.
- Relative links keep Obsidian and validation tooling aligned.
