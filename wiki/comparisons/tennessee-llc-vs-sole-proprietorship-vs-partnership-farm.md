# LLC vs sole proprietorship vs partnership — Tennessee farm framing

**Disclaimer**: **Not legal or tax advice.** This table helps **ask better questions** of **counsel/CPA** for a **profit-intent** Tennessee farm with **possible** employees, **direct sales**, and **two titled parcels** (`SITE_HOME` vs `SITE_FARM`).

**Sources**: UT Extension orientation [`building-a-farm-business-ut-center-of-farm-management.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/building-a-farm-business-ut-center-of-farm-management.md) (**ownership structure impacts taxation, rights/privileges, and liability of owners**); IRS EIN overview [`irs-ein-online-application-overview-capture-2026-04-22.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/irs-ein-online-application-overview-capture-2026-04-22.md) (entity formation sequencing).

| Dimension | Sole proprietorship (SP) | General partnership (GP) | LLC (TN-filed) |
|-----------|---------------------------|---------------------------|----------------|
| **Typical formation** | None with state for pure SP; still may need **local** / **DBA** steps | Written agreement recommended; may file **partnership** forms where applicable | **Articles of Organization** filed with **TN SOS**; operating agreement **recommended** |
| **Liability shape (conceptual)** | Owner and business often **legally undifferentiated** for general obligations—**insurance** still essential | Partners can bind partnership; **joint/several** risk patterns—**counsel** | **Corporate veil** is **not automatic**—operating discipline + **separate books** matter; still need **insurance** |
| **Tax reporting (high level)** | Often **Schedule F** on **1040** per UT page’s pointer—**CPA** | Partnership return + K-1s—**CPA** | Choose **tax classification** (disregarded/partnership/corp) with **CPA** |
| **EIN** | If **no employees** and certain banks may still want EIN—**IRS rules** | Typically **needs EIN** | Typically **needs EIN**; IRS says form **state entity first** online ([`irs-ein-online-application-overview-capture-2026-04-22.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/irs-ein-online-application-overview-capture-2026-04-22.md)) |
| **Two-site fit** | Simplest early **hobby-scale** testing; gets heavy with **contracts**, **vehicles**, **employees** | Useful when **multiple family capital** contributors want explicit **profit share** | Common when **operating** business is **separate** from **personal** and **land** titles are messy |

## Decision gates (professional)

- **Hire counsel** before: **written profit-sharing among non-spouses**, **leased land**, **on-farm processing**, **agritourism**, **external investors**, **creditor financing**.
- **Hire CPA** before: crossing **hobby vs business** IRS facts, choosing **LLC tax class**, interpreting **TNTAP** nexus and **sales tax** on inputs/products.
