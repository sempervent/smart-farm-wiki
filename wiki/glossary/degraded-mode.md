# Degraded mode (automation)

A **degraded mode** is a defined **partial failure** state: automation, network, or power is not fully available, but **safe** manual or reduced-automation operation continues. The wiki separates **failure class** → **manual safe state** → **who acts** in SOPs and runbooks—**not** “silent best effort” when water, animals, or high voltage are involved.

**See:** [`Automation degraded modes and manual fallback SOP`](../analyses/automation-degraded-modes-manual-fallback-sop.md), [`Manual fallback — critical operations`](../analyses/manual-fallback-degraded-modes-critical-operations.md), runbooks under [`Two-site smart farm operations`](../topics/two-site-smart-farm-operations.md).
