# Entity vs site role

- **Entity**: A **named** wiki page under `entities/` (person, org, product, **modeled place**, infrastructure system). It is **stable** navigation—not a deed.
- **Site role** (**`SITE_HOME`** / **`SITE_FARM`**): A **planning label** for **what the parcel does** in the East Tennessee scenario (~5 ac vs ~120 ac). Same role could apply to different deeds in another telling.
- **Named place** (e.g. **Claxton**, **Demory**): A **geographic anchor** for county context and routing—still **not** a legal abstract.

**See:** [`Farm parcels and land units`](../entities/farm-parcels-and-land-units.md), [`Structural audit — page ownership`](../analyses/structural-audit-page-ownership-entity-gaps-and-hub-routing.md).
