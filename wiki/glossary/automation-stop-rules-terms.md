# Automation stop rules (terms)

The **business plan** uses **named stops** so automation does not outrun **labor**, **WAN cost**, or **off-grid** energy reality. Labels (**NS**, **MV**, **CS**) are **gates**—**freeze** or **descope** until evidence exists.

**Examples (not exhaustive):** **CS-5** freezes new always-on **farm** RF/WAN scope while **DR-1** is open; **MV-8** is the **off-grid** local-only / SOC-stressed drill.

**See:** [`Automation stop rules — two-site smart farm`](../analyses/automation-stop-rules-two-site-smart-farm.md), [`Off-grid infrastructure stop rules — Demory`](../analyses/off-grid-operational-decision-rules-power-and-networking-demory-farm.md).
