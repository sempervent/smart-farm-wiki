# Labor and coverage (two-site)

**Coverage** is **who** can respond when **animals**, **water**, or **alerts** demand same-day presence at **`SITE_FARM`** while **`SITE_HOME`** still runs. The wiki uses **matrices** and **hour caps** (e.g. triage vs trip savings) to stop **sensor sprawl** from exceeding **family** time.
