# Distance tax (two-site)

The **distance tax** is the **ongoing cost** of splitting life and work across **`SITE_HOME`** and **`SITE_FARM`**: drive time, fuel, mental load, **batching** constraints, and **coverage** gaps when only one adult can be at the farm. The business plan uses rules like **`DT-*`** and **`R_SPLIT`** to force honest scheduling—**not** “we’ll just pop over.”

**See:** [`Two-site operating model — 5 ac / 120 ac`](../analyses/two-site-operations-model-5ac-homebase-120ac-production.md), [`Trip batching and site visit policy`](../analyses/trip-batching-and-site-visit-policy-two-site-smart-farm.md). **Sites**: [`Two-site sites (SITE_HOME / SITE_FARM)`](two-site-sites.md).
