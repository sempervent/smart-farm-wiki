# Two-site sites (SITE_HOME and SITE_FARM)

In this wiki’s **East Tennessee** scenario, **`SITE_HOME`** names the **~5-acre** **homestead / control-center** parcel (residence, network head-end, workshop, often the **broker** and **records** UI). **`SITE_FARM`** names the **~120-acre** **primary production** parcel (livestock, crops, revenue-facing infrastructure).

**`COMMUTE_ONE_WAY`** (planning default ~**35 minutes**) is the typical one-way drive between them—used for **batching**, **coverage**, and **emergency response** math, not for radio latency. **Colloquial term**: [`Distance tax (two-site)`](distance-tax-two-site.md).
