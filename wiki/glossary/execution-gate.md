# Execution gate

An **execution gate** is a **phase** or **milestone** checkpoint in the **business plan** (often labeled **G1**, **G2**, **G3**) that says when the family may **commit** to the next class of spend or enterprise scale—**after** stated **evidence** exists. It is **not** the same as a single **validation task** (**V***).

**See:** [`Validation backlog and decision gates`](../analyses/east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-validation-backlog.md), [`Execution dossier — Phase 0–1`](../analyses/execution-dossier-hub-phase-0-1-east-tennessee.md), [`Validation gate`](validation-gate.md) (contrast).
