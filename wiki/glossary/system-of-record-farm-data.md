# System of record (farm data)

The **system of record (SoR)** is the place a **defined category** of information is **authoritative** for decisions and audit: e.g. long-term **asset** history, **financial** books, **legal** parcel boundaries. **Telemetry streams**, **dashboards**, and **alerts** are often **not** the SoR unless explicitly designed that way—they can be **stale**, **lossy**, or **ephemeral**.
