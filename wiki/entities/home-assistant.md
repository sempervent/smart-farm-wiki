# Home Assistant

**Home Assistant** is an open-source **home automation** platform that aggregates devices, automations, and dashboards—often self-hosted. In this vault it appears as a **consumer of telemetry** (e.g. MQTT), a **convenience UI**, and sometimes a **non-authoritative** view relative to long-term farm records; see analyses on **system of record** boundaries.

**Official project**: [Home Assistant](https://www.home-assistant.io/) (external).

**Why an entity page**

- Cited in **field telemetry**, **LoRa/MQTT** explorations, **homelab**, and **off-grid smart home** topics; distinct from the **concept** pages for specific radio stacks or protocols.
