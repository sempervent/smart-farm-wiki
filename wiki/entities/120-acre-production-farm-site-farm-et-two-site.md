# 120-acre production farm (SITE_FARM) — East Tennessee two-site model

**What this entity is**: The **planning role** of the **~120-acre** **production holding** in the **East Tennessee two-site** scenario: **primary** locus for **enterprise bets** (livestock, forage, crops, revenue-facing infrastructure), **field telemetry**, and **most** **eyes-on** work that cannot live at the homestead.

**Why it matters**: **Distance**, **darkness**, and **coverage** rules are written against **`SITE_FARM`**; instrumentation priority and water/power CAPEX often bind here first.

**Established in wiki**: ~**120 acres**, **East Tennessee**, separated from `SITE_HOME` by **~35 minutes** one-way (planning default)—see [`Two-site operating model — 5-acre home base and 120-acre farm`](../analyses/two-site-operations-model-5ac-homebase-120ac-production.md).
