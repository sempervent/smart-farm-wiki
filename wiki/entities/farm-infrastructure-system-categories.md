# Farm infrastructure system categories

## Purpose

Cross-site **taxonomy** for **infrastructure** line items in inventories and capital plans: **water movement**, **electrical / on-site power**, **fencing and lanes**, **structures**, **roads and access**, and **communications / telemetry**—linking matrix rows to **entity** pages where they exist.

## Scope

- **In scope**: Category definitions; pointers to **baseline** worksheets.
- **Out of scope**: Parcel-specific measurements (those stay in site baselines and evidence packages).

## Knowns

- Dedicated **entity** pages already exist for [`Farm water infrastructure system`](farm-water-infrastructure-system.md) and [`Farm on-site power system`](farm-on-site-power-system.md).
- **Field telemetry** is its own end-to-end entity ([`Field telemetry network — two-site`](field-telemetry-network-two-site.md)).

## Assumptions

- Categories may **overlap** in the field (e.g. a pump is power + water); inventories still assign a **primary** bucket for planning.
