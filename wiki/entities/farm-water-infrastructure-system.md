# Farm water infrastructure system

**What this entity is**: **Farm-scale water movement and storage** as an **infrastructure system**: sources (well, surface, municipal), **pumps**, **tanks**, **distribution** (pipe, valves), **irrigation** emitters, and **livestock** drinkers—considered together because failures are **welfare** and **crop** critical.

**Why it matters**: Two-site ops and **validation backlogs** treat **water** as a **gate** for scaling livestock or irrigation; telemetry (tank level, flow, pressure) is often **high ROI** but still subordinate to **physical** redundancy and **manual** fallback.

**Canonical analyses / runbooks**: [`Manual fallback for irrigation, gates, and pumps`](../analyses/runbook-manual-fallback-irrigation-gates-pumps.md), [`Manual fallback and degraded modes — critical operations`](../analyses/manual-fallback-degraded-modes-critical-operations.md); business plan **capital** chapters for sequencing.

**Does not claim**: GPM, head, or pipe sizing for your farm—those require engineering and site data.
