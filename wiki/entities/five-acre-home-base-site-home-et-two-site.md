# Five-acre home base (SITE_HOME) — East Tennessee two-site model

**What this entity is**: The **planning role** of the **~5-acre** **homestead / family base** in the **East Tennessee two-site** scenario—not a survey of a particular deed. It is the **default locus** for **residence**, **long-run command infrastructure** (network head-end, broker, backups, workshop/lab), and **planning** when the **120-acre** parcel is the primary production surface.

**Why it matters**: Capital, commute batching, and **telemetry authority** decisions assume **where** “home” operations live vs **where** field risk lives. Confusing `SITE_HOME` with **production land** breaks CAPEX and SoR discussions.

**Established in wiki (not site-specific measurements)**: ~**5 acres**, **East Tennessee**, **~35 min** one-way to production parcel—see [`Two-site operating model — 5-acre home base and 120-acre farm`](../analyses/two-site-operations-model-5ac-homebase-120ac-production.md).

**Place + county anchors (vault)**: **Claxton**, Anderson County — [`Claxton home base (place)`](claxton-home-base.md); county operating object [`Anderson County, Tennessee (two-site context)`](anderson-county-tennessee.md). **Evidence synthesis** (not the deed): [`Claxton home base — site intelligence`](../analyses/claxton-home-base-site-intelligence.md).

**Does not claim**: Exact boundary lines, addresses, or zoning; those belong in private records or future **parcel** evidence in `raw/`.
