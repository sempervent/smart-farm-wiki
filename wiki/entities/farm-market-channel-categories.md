# Farm market channel categories

## Purpose

Stable **buckets** for how product reaches buyers—used so revenue and enterprise analyses can say “direct retail” or “wholesale” without redefining channels each time.

## Scope

- **In scope**: Typical channel **types** relevant to the vault’s enterprise discussions (production-led vs alternate scenarios).
- **Out of scope**: **Your** customer list, pricing, or contracted volumes.

## Knowns

- The ET **enterprise options** and **recommended strategy** analyses compare paths (e.g. grazing-led vs deferred options); **agritourism** is treated as a **different scenario package** with its own hub.

## Assumptions

- Most enterprises use **multiple** channels; the buckets are for **planning**, not exclusivity.
