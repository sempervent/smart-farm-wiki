# farmOS

**farmOS** is an open-source **web-based farm management, planning, and record-keeping** application built on **Drupal**, commonly self-hosted. In this vault it is the **canonical named product** for rich **asset** and **log** models, geometry-friendly records, and integration discussions—not a generic synonym for “farm database.”

**Official project**: [farmOS.org](https://farmOS.org/) (external).

**Why an entity page**

- Referenced across **telemetry SoR**, **spatial registry**, **comparisons**, and **topic** hubs; a single **entity** avoids repeating product definition on every analysis.
