# Labor roles — two-site farm operations

## Purpose

Generic **labor buckets** used in the **two-site** business-plan artifacts: who holds **primary** operating responsibility, who provides **secondary / family** capacity, where **seasonal** or **contract** labor might plug in, and how **coverage windows** interact with the **distance tax**—without duplicating full schedules.

## Scope

- **In scope**: Role **definitions** and links to matrices / rhythms.
- **Out of scope**: Named individuals’ hours, wages, or employment contracts.

## Knowns

- The ET package uses **family labor** and **weekly rhythm** analyses as canonical narrative.
- **Coverage** is a first-class operational constraint alongside capital and telemetry.

## Assumptions

- Roles are **overlapping** in real life; the buckets are for **planning** clarity, not HR precision.
