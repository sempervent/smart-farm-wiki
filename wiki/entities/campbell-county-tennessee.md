# Campbell County, Tennessee (two-site context)

**What this entity is**: A **county-level operating object** for the East Tennessee **two-site** package — **survey statistics** plus **one vault WSS session** used in **Demory** intelligence. It is **not** a complete soils map of every acre in the county, and it is **not** your deed.

---

## Role in the two-site system

- **Jurisdiction bucket** for **`SITE_FARM`** at [`Demory farm site (place)`](demory-farm-site.md): NASS **row**, and **Campbell WSS AOI** evidence used cautiously in [`Demory farm — site intelligence`](../analyses/demory-farm-site-intelligence.md).
- **Paired comparison**: Read with [`Anderson County, Tennessee (two-site context)`](anderson-county-tennessee.md) in [`Anderson County vs Campbell County — two-site operating implications`](../comparisons/anderson-county-vs-campbell-county-operating-implications.md).
