# Vision, objectives, and constraints

**Hub**: [`Planning framework`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-framework.md)

## Vision (10 years)

Build a **for-profit**, **two-site** farm business in **East Tennessee**: **~120 acres** as **primary production**, **~5 acres** as **family home + control center** (smart house, workshop/lab, telemetry spine). Move from **side income** to **credible salary-replacement cash** only if **gates** and **margins** justify **reducing off-farm work**.

## Objectives (ranked)

| Rank | Objective | Measurable proxy |
|------|-----------|------------------|
| 1 | **Profit** | Operating margin after **full-cost** allocation (incl. **labor at replacement wage**) |
| 2 | **Low labor** | Owner hours per $ gross; **peak-week** survivability |
| 3 | **Sustainability / resilience** | Shock survival (drought, price dip) without **liquidity** failure |

## Hard constraints

| ID | Constraint |
|----|------------|
| C1 | **5 ac** = **not** primary commercial production land |
| C2 | **120 ac** = **primary** land-based revenue |
| C3 | **~35 min** one-way between sites (planning basis) |
| C4 | **Family labor** primary—**no** implicit hired **resident** manager |
| C5 | **Minimal** early spend; **phased** CAPEX; **selective** infrastructure financing |
| C6 | **Automation** is **budgeted** (install, maintain, false positives, cyber) |

## Success criteria (by phase gate)

| Gate | Pass condition (fill numbers locally) |
|------|--------------------------------------|
| End Phase 1 | **Land/water/legal** unknowns **bounded** (measured, deferred with owner, or waived in writing); **pilot** telemetry **one** green light—see [`Execution gates — Phase 0–1`](execution-gates-phase-0-1-east-tennessee-two-site.md) **EG-LEV** / **EG-SITE** |
| End Phase 2 | **Positive operating cash** **or** **honest** subsidy budget with **dated** end |
| End Phase 3 | **Margin** supports **scale** **or** **second** stream without **heroic** labor |
| Phase 4 | **Farm cash** ≥ **X%** of **household need** for **Y** consecutive years |

## Stop rules (pause capital / pivot)

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Two** consecutive years **negative** operating cash (after **replacement-wage** labor charge) | **Freeze** non-essential CAPEX; **cut** herd or **raise** lease share |
| **Automation OPEX** > **documented** benefit (trips avoided + risk value) | **De-scope** sensors; **consolidate** stack |
| **Key person** unavailable **> N** weeks | **Reduce** livestock exposure; **activate** degraded SOPs |
| **Debt service** > **D%** of **gross** farm revenue | **No new** term debt without **written** recovery plan |

## Non-goals

- **Lifestyle branding** as substitute for **unit economics**
- **Maximum sensor count** without **registry + triage**
- **Single-enterprise** lock-in **before** Phase 1 **validation**

## Evidence and doctrine alignment (2026-04)

**Phase 1 “bounded unknowns”** in the success table means **honest closure or deferral** of register items—not “we read the wiki.” Route **desk** evidence (county NASS bands, WSS-derived terrain bias, portals) through [`Local evidence package`](../topics/local-evidence-package-east-tennessee-two-site.md) and **parcel truth** through [`Claxton and Demory — missing data register`](claxton-and-demory-missing-data-register.md) + [`Field verification program — Phase 0–1`](field-verification-program-phase-0-1-claxton-demory.md). **Ledger**: [`Business plan integration revision audit (2026-04)`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-integration-revision-audit-2026-04.md).

## Assumptions

| ID | Assumption | Review by |
|----|------------|-----------|
| A1 | **35 min** is **typical** one-way for **batch** planning | Seasonal drive log |
| A2 | **At least one** family member can do **surge** weeks (calving, hay weather) | Annual calendar |
| A3 | **Off-farm income** bridges **Years 1–3** | Household budget |

**Execution risk**: **A2** and **A3** are **wishful** until **evidence**. **A2** is **illusory** without **V11** time log **plus** **one** **documented** **surge** window and **coverage** that **does not** assume **heroics**. **A3** is **illusory** without **named** income sources and **stress** if **job** **loss** **or** **health** **event**—the “bridge” is **not** automatic.

### Emotional scaling (explicit non-goal)

**Profit intent** does **not** protect you from **scale-before-proof** under stress. If **stop rules** in this page would be **emotionally** “too harsh” to follow in public, **the plan is not operational**—it is **theater** ([`Ready for execution`](execution-dossier-hub-phase-0-1-east-tennessee.md#ready-for-execution-the-plan-is-operational)).
