# Capital plan and phased infrastructure roadmap

**Hub**: [`Planning framework`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-framework.md)  
**Two-site economics**: [`CAPEX, OPEX, and enterprise sequencing — two-site constraint`](capex-opex-enterprise-sequencing-two-site-constraint.md)

## Capital principles

1. **No** **irreversible** **dirt** **or** **fence** **before** **Phase** **1** **land** **intelligence** **unless** **no-regret** (legal access, erosion, **acute** **water**).
2. **Finance** **durable** **infrastructure** **selectively** (fence, **well**, **power** **drops**)—**not** **consumables** **or** **gadgets**.
3. **Homestead** **brain** **CAPEX** **in** **small** **increments**—**reuse** **homelab** **discipline**.
4. **Match** **debt** **service** **to** **Phase** **2+** **margin** **evidence**—**not** **hope**.

## Infrastructure sequencing (grazing-led family)

| Sequence # | Item | Enables |
|------------|------|---------|
| 1 | **Legal** **boundary**, **access**, **use** **clarity** | **Everything** |
| 2 | **Water** **development** **+** **distribution** | **Grazing** **density** |
| 3 | **Perimeter** **+** **interior** **fence** **plan** | **Rotation** |
| 4 | **Handling** **facility** **minimal** **viable** | **Marketing**, **vet** |
| 5 | **Trailer** **/** **truck** **class** **adequate** | **Revenue** **realization** |
| 6 | **Telemetry** **backbone** **after** **manual** **proof** | **Trip** **reduction** |

## Phased capital table

| Phase | Infrastructure | CAPEX posture | Financing caution |
|-------|----------------|---------------|---------------------|
| **0** | **Maps**, **docs**, **basic** **tools**, **comms** | **Minimal** | **No** **term** **debt** **for** **toys** |
| **1** | **Soil/water** **tests**, **fence** **repair** **patches**, **pilot** **sensor** | **Low**; **grants**/EQIP-class **if** **eligible** **(not** **assumed** **)** | **Match** **any** **loan** **to** **validated** **lease** **or** **off-farm** **income** |
| **2** | **Water** **full** **plan**, **fence** **as** **designed**, **corral** **MVP**, **herd** **or** **stock** **purchase** | **Moderate**; **livestock** **as** **inventory** **where** **appropriate** | **Cattle** **price** **risk**—**size** **herd** **to** **liquidity** |
| **3** | **Scale** **rotation**, **optional** **hay** **gear** **or** **custom** **hire**, **marketing** **build** | **Selective** **financing** **on** **depreciable** **with** **margin** | **Equipment** **traps**—**prefer** **hire** **until** **hours**/year **clear** |
| **4** | **Cold** **chain**, **direct** **sales** **assets**, **redundant** **comms** | **Tied** **to** **salary** **replacement** **proof** | **Avoid** **stretching** **for** **image** |
