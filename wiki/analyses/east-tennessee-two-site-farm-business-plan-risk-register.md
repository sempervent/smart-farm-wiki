# Risk register and mitigation strategy

**Hub**: [`Planning framework`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-framework.md)

## Method

- **Likelihood** **×** **impact** **=** **priority** (1–5 each); **mitigation** **owner** **named** **locally**.
- **Two-site** **and** **telemetry** **risks** **are** **first-class**—not **afterthoughts**.
