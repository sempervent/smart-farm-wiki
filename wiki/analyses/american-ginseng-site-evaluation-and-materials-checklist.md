# American ginseng — site evaluation and materials checklist

**Routing**: Use this page to **score** Appalachian-style woodlots, decide **go / no-go**, and assemble **physical materials** before you commit stratified seed. **Not** a substitute for a **soil test**, **TDEC** harvest rules, or **professional forestry** advice.

**Mandatory vs optional**

| Tier | Scope |
|------|--------|
| **Mandatory** | Complete **shade**, **drainage**, and **access** checks before buying seed; **soil test** for pH/Ca/P on any site above “reject”. |
| **Optional** | Formal **scorecards** below; **3′×3′** test plots before large buys (Beyfuss protocol PDF). |

## 1. Shade and canopy targets

The Cornell-style **one-page** score sheet in [`ginseng-site-selection-capture-inbox-2026-04-20.pdf`](../../raw/processed/2026/ginseng-site-selection-capture-inbox-2026-04-20.pdf) states **American ginseng** wants **≥65% shade** with **~78%** called **optimal** across “most soil types.” Use a **spherical densiometer** or comparable method and **record** readings at midsummer—not under leafless winter canopy.

**Mid-Atlantic matrix** PDF [`ginseng-visual-site-assessment-matrix-capture-inbox-2026-04-20.pdf`](../../raw/processed/2026/ginseng-visual-site-assessment-matrix-capture-inbox-2026-04-20.pdf) reinforces **north / east / northeast** aspect preference and penalizes hot western aspects unless the capture’s exception language applies.

---

## 2. Scorecards (quick use)

### A. Cornell one-pager (dominant trees + exposure + security)

Work from the **printed** PDF: assign points per **dominant overstory**, **proximity / patrol / camera** exposure category, and **wild ginseng indicator** presence. Captured bands: **40–60** excellent; **30–40** potentially good; **20–30** marginal—**full soil test**; **<20** poor—look elsewhere. The sheet explicitly awards points for **“Surveillance camera installed”**—this is a **planning** hint aligned with the **security** companion guide.

### B. Visual matrix (species, slope, plantable fraction)

Categories **A–D** in [`ginseng-visual-site-assessment-matrix-capture-inbox-2026-04-20-extracted.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/ginseng-visual-site-assessment-matrix-capture-inbox-2026-04-20-extracted.md) score **sugar maple / tulip / walnut** bands highest, **slope** 10–30% best, and reward **large-tree** sites with **high plantable fraction**.

---

## 3. Small-scale field test (before scaling)

[`ginseng-testing-potential-woodland-sites-capture-inbox-2026-04-20.pdf`](../../raw/processed/2026/ginseng-testing-potential-woodland-sites-capture-inbox-2026-04-20.pdf) outlines a **protocol**: buy **only stratified** seed; **surface sterilize** with **mild bleach**; **float** seed; rinse, keep moist in baggies; lay out **3 ft × 3 ft** plots; **50 seeds**/plot; rake litter, scratch mineral soil, broadcast, recover mulch. Treat this as a **pilot BOM**—see materials checklist.

---

## 4. Tennessee / soil chemistry cross-check

The **MTSU-region** wild-simulated guide (2026-04-19 ingest) discusses **pH ~5–6**, **calcium**, and **phosphorus** sufficiency with **East Tennessee** citations—after scorecards pass, map soil test results to that PDF’s guidance rather than guessing lime or fertilizer.

---

## 5. When you must involve forestry (PB1799)

If opening canopy, removing midstory, or preparing **regeneration** for **future** hardwood composition, use **UT Extension PB1799** [`ut-extension-pb1799-capture-inbox-2026-04-20.pdf`](../../raw/processed/2026/ut-extension-pb1799-capture-inbox-2026-04-20.pdf) (*Professional Hardwood Notes*—site preparation in natural hardwood stands). **Ginseng** needs **stable shade**; avoid treatments that **suddenly** expose the forest floor without a **reclosure** plan.

## 6. Materials checklist (pilot + first season)

| Item | Role |
|------|------|
| **Stratified seed** (vendor COA / reputation) | Only seed grade appropriate for your chosen method |
| **Field notebook** + private map sketch | Track plots without publishing GPS |
| **Soil probe / zip bags** | Depth and horizon notes for odd wet spots |
| **pH + soil test mailer** | Lab analysis; compare to TN + VCE bands |
| **Densiometer or shade photos + canopy sketch** | Document 65–80%+ shade claim |
| **Steel garden rake, hand rake, flags** | Beyfuss plot layout; plot corners |
| **Digital scale (0.1 g)** + **counting tray** | 50-seed batches; optional float-test bucket |
| **Bleach + timer + rinse water + gloves** | Only if executing sterilization—follow **PDF** concentrations and disposal rules |
| **Breathable seed storage** (baggies / vermiculite per vendor) | Bridge from delivery to planting |
| **Trail camera(s)** (optional Phase 0) | See security companion—mount after plot layout |
