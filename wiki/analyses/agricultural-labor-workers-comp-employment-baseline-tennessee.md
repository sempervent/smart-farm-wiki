# Agricultural labor, workers’ compensation, and employment baseline — Tennessee

## Federal — official overview (DOL)

**Capture**: [`us-dol-wage-hour-agricultural-employment-overview-capture-2026-04-22.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/us-dol-wage-hour-agricultural-employment-overview-capture-2026-04-22.md)

| Law / program | Role |
|---------------|------|
| **FLSA agriculture** | Minimum wage / overtime / recordkeeping / child labor for **covered** agricultural employers—see linked FLSA agriculture page on DOL.gov |
| **MSPA** | Migrant/seasonal worker housing/transport/disclosures; **farm labor contractor** registration |
| **H-2A** | Temporary nonimmigrant ag worker program compliance |
| **Field sanitation** | WHD/OSHA-related minimum sanitation standards in **covered** settings |

**Gate**: DOL states statutes **overlap**—use **WHD district office** for facts.

## Family labor (two-site plan default)

- **Owners + immediate family** working the operation still trigger **some** rules depending on **entity**, **age**, and **tasks**—**CPA + attorney** for youth tasks near equipment and for payroll classification.
