# Farm insurance and liability checklist — two-site Tennessee

**Primary extension source**: UT **W1266** — [`ut-extension-w1266-insurance-considerations-farming-operation-pdf.md`](../source-notes/ut-extension-w1266-insurance-considerations-farming-operation-pdf.md) (PDF + extract).

## A — Official vs educational vs commercial

| Layer | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Official** | Insurance contracts are **private law** between you and carrier—**policy text** wins. |
| **Educational (high authority for learning)** | UT W1266 explains **coverage families** and **agent selection**—still **not** a policy. |
| **Commercial / low** | Third-party agency newsletters in `raw/` (e.g. `what-is-farm-liability-insurance.md`)—**ideas only**. |

## B — Agent question checklist (structured)

Use separate rows for **`SITE_HOME`** vs **`SITE_FARM`** where exposures differ.

| # | Question | Why |
|---|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Does the carrier treat us as **personal farm**, **commercial farm**, or **mixed**? | W1266: not all carriers farm the same |
| 2 | Do **owned vs leased** acres match covered locations? | Two-site distance + multiple parcels |
| 3 | Are **vehicles/equipment** on public roads covered as farm operations? | W1266 warns to **confirm** road coverage |
| 4 | If **agritourism** or **on-farm events** activate, is **commercial GL** required? | W1266 distinguishes **general farm** vs **commercial business** liability |
| 5 | For **direct sales / U-pick / on-farm food**, is **product liability** included or excluded? | W1266 product liability section |
| 6 | **Workers’ comp** vs **Hired help** medical payments—what triggers each? | Cross-read labor baseline |
| 7 | **Cyber** / remote access for telemetry—any **endorsement**? | Vault has separate security doctrine; insurance is additive |
