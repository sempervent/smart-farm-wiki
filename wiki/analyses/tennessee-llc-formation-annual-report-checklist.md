# Tennessee LLC formation and annual report checklist

**Sources**: SOS/TNBEAR URL index [`tennessee-sos-business-services-official-url-index-capture-2026-04-22.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/tennessee-sos-business-services-official-url-index-capture-2026-04-22.md); IRS EIN sequencing [`irs-ein-online-application-overview-capture-2026-04-22.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/irs-ein-online-application-overview-capture-2026-04-22.md).

## A — Official / quasi-official (verify live SOS instructions)

| Step | Action | Notes |
|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Confirm **Name availability** / reservation rules | Use **TNBEAR** entity search from URL index |
| 2 | File **Articles of Organization** per current SOS checklist | Fees/forms **change**—download current PDF/web form |
| 3 | Obtain **registered agent** / registered office | **TN** statutory requirement for LLC—confirm details on SOS |
| 4 | Prepare **Operating agreement** (internal) | **Recommended**; not necessarily filed—**attorney** |
| 5 | **Annual report** | Open **TNBEAR Annual Report Instructions** (URL index) and follow **current** due-date and fee tables—**do not** mirror dynamic numbers here |

## B — Federal sequencing (IRS)

| Step | Action | Notes |
|------|--------|------|
| 6 | **EIN after state formation** for online path | IRS: forming entity at state **first** avoids EIN delay ([`irs-ein-online-application-overview-capture-2026-04-22.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/irs-ein-online-application-overview-capture-2026-04-22.md)) |
| 7 | Name a **person** as **responsible party** | See [`federal-ein-irs-registration-baseline-farm-business.md`](federal-ein-irs-registration-baseline-farm-business.md) |
