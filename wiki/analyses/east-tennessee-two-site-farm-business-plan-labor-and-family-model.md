# Labor model and weekly operating rhythm

**Hub**: [`Planning framework`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-framework.md)  
**Coverage template**: [`Weekly coverage matrix — two-site farm operations`](weekly-coverage-matrix-two-site-farm-operations.md)

## Labor philosophy

- **Family-first**: father, son, grandson as **primary** **capacity**.
- **No** **phantom** **hired** **man**—if **needed**, **budget** **explicitly** in **Phase 3+**.
- **Travel-time-aware**: **batch** **work** **at** **120**; **homestead** **hosts** **planning**, **maintenance**, **comms**.

## Roles (assign real names locally)

| Role | Typical duties | Peak risk |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| **Ops lead** | **Schedule**, **sales**, **vet**, **major** **equipment** | **Burnout** **surge** **weeks** |
| **Field lead** | **Moves**, **water**, **fence**, **handling** | **Injury** |
| **Tech lead** | **Broker**, **sensors**, **backups**, **spares** | **False** **confidence** **in** **dashboards** |

## Weekly rhythm (template — grazing-led)

| DOW | Homestead (~5 ac) | Remote (~120 ac) |
|-----|-------------------|------------------|
| **Mon** | **Plan** **week**; **order** **parts** | **—** **or** **short** **check** if **alert** |
| **Tue** | **Shop** / **print** **parts** | **Batch** **visit**: **rotate**, **water**, **eyes** |
| **Wed** | **Books** / **records** | **—** |
| **Thu** | **Telemetry** **review**; **firmware** | **Batch** **visit** |
| **Fri** | **Family** **admin** | **—** **or** **preflight** **weekend** **work** |
| **Sat** | **Optional** **deep** **work** | **Heavy** **tasks** **if** **needed** |
| **Sun** | **Rest** / **exception** | **Calving** **season** **override** |

**Rule**: **Minimum** **2** **batched** **120** **visits**/week in **Phase 2+** **grazing**—**adjust** **up** **in** **surge**.

## Seasonal surge calendar (placeholders)

| Window | Surge | Mitigation |
|--------|-------|------------|
| **Calving** (if cow-calf) | **Daily** **possible** | **Temporary** **stay** **near** **120** **or** **reduce** **herd** **complexity** |
| **Hay** | **Weather** **windows** | **Custom** **hire** **or** **partner**—**avoid** **solo** **equipment** **trap** |
| **Weaning / shipping** | **Trailer** **hours** | **Pre-book** **buyer**/**auction** |

## Telemetry interaction with labor

| Telemetry | Labor effect |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Tank OK** | **Skip** **trip** |
| **Tank bad** | **Dispatch** **batched** **or** **emergency** |
| **False alarm** | **Costs** **labor** **via** **triage**—**budget** **time** **in** **weekly** **rhythm** |

## Phased labor expectations

| Phase | Expectation |
|-------|-------------|
| **0** | **Low** **hours**; **paperwork** **and** **maps** |
| **1** | **Field** **days** **2–4×**/month **+** **surveys** |
| **2** | **Part-time** **farm** **job** **level** **(0.3–0.5 FTE** **family** **combined**) **typical** |
| **3** | **Scale** **or** **hire** **fraction**—**explicit** |
| **4** | **Possible** **full-time** **equivalent** **on** **farm** **if** **margin** **supports** |
