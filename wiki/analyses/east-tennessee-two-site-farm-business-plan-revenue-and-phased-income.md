# Revenue model and business milestones

**Hub**: [`Planning framework`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-framework.md)  
**Strategy**: [`Recommended enterprise strategy`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-recommended-enterprise-strategy.md)

## Revenue architecture

| Stream | Phase | Role |
|--------|-------|------|
| **Lease / contract** (hunting, cash rent, custom graze) | **1–2** | **Supplemental** **cash**; **reduces** **need** **for** **early** **herd** |
| **Grazing** (livestock sales) | **2–4** | **Core** **operating** **business** |
| **Hay / value-add** | **3+** | **Only** **if** **equipment** **or** **hire** **solved** |
| **Direct** **premium** | **3–4** | **Margin** **lift**—**not** **required** **to** **start** |

## Short-term supplemental vs long-term salary replacement

| Horizon | Target | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| **Y1–2** | **Supplemental**: **lease**, **small** **trades**, **minimal** **livestock** **after** **gates** | **Off-farm** **income** **dominant** |
| **Y3–5** | **Growing** **operating** **margin**; **family** **hours** **tracked** | **Salary** **replacement** **not** **claimed** **without** **2+** **yr** **track** |
| **Y6–10** | **Salary** **replacement** **architecture** | **Household** **cash** **from** **farm** **≥** **target** **%** **(set** **number** **)** |
