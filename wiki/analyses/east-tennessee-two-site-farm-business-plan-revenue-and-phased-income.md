# Revenue model and milestones

## Purpose

Describe **how money is expected to enter** the farm business over **~10 years**: **supplemental** cash first, then **operating margin discipline**, then—**only if metrics support it**—a **salary-replacement band** for the **household**. **Enterprise details** on the **120-acre** parcel may still move (stocker vs cow-calf, lease share, etc.); this page defines **streams**, **horizons**, and **milestones** without inventing **your** **household budget**.

## Revenue architecture (strategy family)

| Stream | Phase | Role |
|--------|-------|------|
| Lease / contract (hunting, cash rent, custom graze) | 1–2 | **Supplemental** cash; can **reduce** early owned-herd pressure |
| Grazing (livestock sales) | 2–4 | **Core** operating business on **120 ac** |
| Hay / value-add | 3+ | Only if **equipment or hire** is **solved** |
| Direct premium | 3–4 | **Margin lift**—not required to **start** |

## Horizons

| Horizon | Target character | Notes |
|---------|------------------|-------|
| Y1–2 | **Supplemental**: lease, small trades, minimal livestock **after gates** | Off-farm income **dominant** for most households |
| Y3–5 | **Growing** operating margin; labor hours **tracked** | Do **not** claim salary replacement without **2+ years** of **books** you trust |
| Y6–10 | **Salary-replacement architecture** **if** sustained | **Household** cash from farm ≥ **target %** of need—**fill number locally** |
