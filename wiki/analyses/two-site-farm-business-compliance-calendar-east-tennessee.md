# Two-site farm business compliance calendar — East Tennessee

**Purpose**: **Recurring** reminders for **`SITE_HOME` (Claxton / Anderson)** + **`SITE_FARM` (Demory / Campbell)** operations. **Confirm dates** on official sites annually—especially **TNBEAR**, **Revenue**, and **FinCEN**.

## Calendar table (template — fill with your fiscal year)

| When | Item | Authority / pointer |
|------|------|---------------------|
| **Jan 1** | **Assessment date** for many TN property tax / greenbelt continuity rules | [`greenbelt.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/greenbelt.md) |
| **Jan–Mar** | Prepare **greenbelt** / use-value applications | **March 15** first-time deadline per [`greenbelt.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/greenbelt.md) |
| **Quarterly** | **FinCEN BOI** posture check | [`Beneficial ownership information reporting — FinCEN / CTA status`](beneficial-ownership-information-reporting-fincen-status.md) |
| **After FYE** | **Business tax** due **15th day of 4th month** after fiscal year end (Revenue excerpt) | [`tennessee-business-tax-registration-licensing-tntap.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/tennessee-business-tax-registration-licensing-tntap.md) |
| **Per SOS fiscal year** | **LLC annual report** window per **TNBEAR** instructions | SOS URL index [`tennessee-sos-business-services-official-url-index-capture-2026-04-22.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/tennessee-sos-business-services-official-url-index-capture-2026-04-22.md) |
| **Ongoing** | **County business license** renewal / receipts thresholds | TNTAP excerpt + **county clerk** |
| **Annual** | **Insurance** renewal review with agent using W1266 checklist | [`Farm insurance and liability checklist`](farm-insurance-liability-checklist-two-site-tennessee.md) |

## Two-site nuance

- Some items are **per parcel/county** (greenbelt, property assessment), others **per operating entity** (EIN, TNTAP, BOI).
