# Starting and stocking a pond as a beautiful water feature (query synthesis)

**Question**: How do you **start** and **stock** your own **pond** and turn it into a **beautiful water feature**?

**Disclaimer**: **Damming**, **grading**, and **stocking** fish can be **regulated** (federal/state **waters**, **invasive species**, **health** rules). Use official agencies—not blog summaries—as the authority for your site.

## 1. Pick your scale: garden pond vs. impoundment

- **Smaller, lined backyard ponds** — Excavation, **liner**, **filtration** (mechanical + **biofilter** / plant zones), **circulation**, **edging**, and **planting** for beauty and habitat. A long DIY walkthrough with **self-cleaning biofilter** logic and ecosystem notes is in [`tyrant-farms-diy-backyard-pond-self-cleaning-biofilter.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/tyrant-farms-diy-backyard-pond-self-cleaning-biofilter.md) (duck-oriented; principles transfer to ornamental fish).
- **Farm ponds / larger impoundments** — Site **watershed**, **NRCS** guidance, **erosion** control, sometimes **liners** where soils leak; overview framing in [`plant-a-fountain-dig-a-pond-acreage-life.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/plant-a-fountain-dig-a-pond-acreage-life.md).

## 2. Beauty: sound, motion, light, plants

- **Moving water** — Waterfalls and spillways add **sound** and **aeration**; forum tips on **liner**, **stone**, and **grading** appear in [`backyard-ponds-farm-waterfall-forum-short.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/backyard-ponds-farm-waterfall-forum-short.md).
- **Fountains / bubblers** — Discussed as **oxygen** and **visual** upgrades in [`plant-a-fountain-dig-a-pond-acreage-life.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/plant-a-fountain-dig-a-pond-acreage-life.md); landscape designer quotes in the same piece.
- **Design culture** — Historical/design context (non-how-to) in [`water-features-origins-history-design.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/water-features-origins-history-design.md).

## 3. Water quality before “pretty”

Algae blooms, odor, and dead fish are not beautiful. The **Tyrant Farms** piece emphasizes **oxygen**, **aerobic** biology, and **biofiltration** rather than chemical-only maintenance ([`tyrant-farms-diy-backyard-pond-self-cleaning-biofilter.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/tyrant-farms-diy-backyard-pond-self-cleaning-biofilter.md)). **Rip-rap**, **grassed waterways**, and **erosion** control matter on larger ponds ([`plant-a-fountain-dig-a-pond-acreage-life.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/plant-a-fountain-dig-a-pond-acreage-life.md)).

## 4. Stocking fish (and legal checks)

The **Acreage Life** capture advises **commercial dealers**, species suited to **your area**, and **state natural resources** approval—explicit warning on **prohibited** species ([`plant-a-fountain-dig-a-pond-acreage-life.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/plant-a-fountain-dig-a-pond-acreage-life.md)). **Balance** stocking with **volume**, **aeration**, and **predator** realities (herons, raccoons—general knowledge; **confidence: medium**).

## 5. Moving water for irrigation (adjacent)

If the pond doubles as a **water source** for **gardens**, see pumping/siphon threads [`how-to-make-water-go-uphill-without-electricity.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/how-to-make-water-go-uphill-without-electricity.md), [`pump-water-uphill.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/pump-water-uphill.md), and [`pond-pump-uphill-hose-bib-irrigation-reddit.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/pond-pump-uphill-hose-bib-irrigation-reddit.md)—hydraulics and **practical** pitfalls, not a substitute for pump sizing with a qualified vendor.
