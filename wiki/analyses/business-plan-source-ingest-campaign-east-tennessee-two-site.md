# Business plan source-ingest campaign — East Tennessee two-site package

**Scope**: Minimum **authoritative** sources to **reduce uncertainty** on the completed **ET two-site business plan** package—not maximum volume.

**Anchors**: [`Planning framework`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-framework.md), [`Remediation backlog`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-remediation-backlog.md), [`Hostile internal review`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-hostile-internal-review.md).

**Principles**: Prefer **primary** and **Extension / agency** documents over generic blogs. Optimize for **decision support** (gates, numeric bands, legal triggers). **Do not** ingest broad inspirational or “smart farm vision” material unless it directly changes a **gate**, **budget line**, or **risk owner**.

---

## 1. Campaign goals

| Goal | Success looks like |
|------|-------------------|
| **G1 — Enterprise economics** | At least one **TN-relevant** enterprise budget or **budget methodology** per **major** enterprise path under consideration, with **line items** mappable to [`revenue model`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-revenue-and-phased-income.md) and [`capital plan`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-capital-and-financing.md). |
| **G2 — Production + conservation physics** | **Grazing / forage / water** guidance sufficient to defend **carrying capacity bands**, **fence + water sequencing**, and **NRCS practice** choices in [`recommended strategy`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-recommended-enterprise-strategy.md) and [`capital phasing`](capital-phasing-table-years-0-10-two-site-smart-farm.md). |
| **G3 — Compliance surface** | **County / state** pointers for **structures**, **transport**, **on-farm sales / agritourism** (if ever on-path), captured as **citable** notes—enough to stop treating **V8** as a wiki placeholder. |
| **G4 — Telemetry as OPEX** | **farmOS** (or chosen stack) **data model + ops** docs grounded so [`smart-tech strategy`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-smart-tech-strategy.md) and [`instrumentation matrix`](instrumentation-priority-matrix-two-site-smart-farm.md) avoid fantasy schemas and double-counted labor. |
| **G5 — Downside protection** | **Insurance / liability** primers (rural or farm-specific where possible) tied to **binder review** and **Phase 2** gates in [`risk register`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-risk-register.md) and [`validation backlog`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-validation-backlog.md). |
| **G6 — Market realism** | **Channel economics** references (auctions, direct, processor) to support **all-in per head** and **processing path** rows—not optimistic price decks. |
| **G7 — Books** | **IRS / extension** farm recordkeeping and **Schedule F–adjacent** framing so “margin” claims in the wiki stay **defensible** (not tax advice—**decision-grade clarity**). |

**Stop rule**: If a candidate source does not advance **G1–G7** for a **named** wiki section or validation ID (**V1–V11**), **defer** or **skip**.

---

## 2. Prioritized source categories (order of attack)

**P0** — Must land before **creditable** scaling narrative: **TN/ET enterprise budgets**, **Extension production guides (forage/beef/water)**, **NRCS grazing + water + conservation planning**, **local regulatory capture** (zoning / structures / transport).

**P1** — Before **telemetry fleet** or **salary** claims: **farmOS documentation**, **telemetry architecture** (OPEX, alerts, integration boundaries), **market-channel economics**, **insurance / liability**.

**P2** — When enterprise path includes **on-farm sales / events / lodging**: **agritourism regulatory + liability**; otherwise **keep as optional** backlog items, not bulk ingest.

**P3** — **Tax / accounting** references: small set of **stable** IRS + Extension primers; **avoid** deep tax strategy content in wiki unless explicitly scoped.

## 5. Rules — raw note vs source note vs synthesis vs comparison

| Stage | When to use | Repository action |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| **`raw/` only** | You have a **file** (PDF, export, **your** scan of a county doc) but **no** time to summarize; **must** preserve **provenance**. | Add under `raw/inbox/` → move to `raw/processed/...`; run **`pdf_to_markdown.py`** when PDF; **do not** rewrite processed text to “fix” meaning (per [`ingest workflow`](../../docs/workflows/ingest.md)). |
| **`source_note`** | The source is **stable** enough to **cite** (URL with archive date, government PDF, manual version); you can state **what it answers** for the wiki. | Create **`wiki/source-notes/<stem>.md`** with `page_type: source_note`, **`source_ids`**, **scope**, **limits**, **link** to raw extract. |
| **Synthesis update** | A **named** business-plan section needs a **number**, **gate**, **or** **risk row** **backed** by the source. | Edit **specific** analysis pages (e.g. [`capital plan`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-capital-and-financing.md)); add **“Source:”** line with **relative** link to source-note; update [`remediation backlog`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-remediation-backlog.md) if weakness closes. |
| **Comparison input** | Two **credible** **alternatives** (hire vs own, auction vs direct, farmOS vs ledger-only) need **side-by-side** criteria **not** buried in narrative. | Create or update **`wiki/comparisons/*.md`** (or **dedicated** analysis table) with **explicit** columns; **each** column should trace to **source-notes** or **labeled** owner assumptions. |
| **Defer** | Source is **motivational**, **duplicate**, or **answers** a question **already** closed by **validation** in the field. | **No** new file; optional **one-line** pointer in [`validation backlog`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-validation-backlog.md) under **deferred**. |

**Escalation shortcut**: If ingest would **change** **financing** or **legal** posture, require **both** a **source_note** **and** a **log** entry (`ingest`) with **gate ID** affected.
