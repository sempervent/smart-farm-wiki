# Tennessee greenbelt and property tax — two-site Claxton and Demory

**Primary capture**: [`greenbelt.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/greenbelt.md) — **Tennessee Comptroller / State Board of Equalization** greenbelt program text (Agricultural, Forest, Open Space Act; **T.C.A.** §§ **67-5-1001** et seq. cited in capture).

## Official requirements (from capture — verify with assessor)

| Topic | Statement in capture | Implication |
|-------|------------------------|-------------|
| **Application** | Owner must **file application** with **assessor** in **county where property is located** | **Anderson** application for **Claxton** parcel(s); **Campbell** for **Demory** parcel(s)—separate workflows |
| **First-time deadline** | **March 15** for first-time agricultural/forest/open space | Miss triggers **disqualification** risk until late rules apply |
| **Recording** | Approved apps must be **recorded** with **Register of Deeds**; **recording fee** | Budget + clerk coordination |
| **Reapplication** | Not required unless **ownership as of Jan 1** changes | Track deed changes carefully |
| **Agricultural land definition** | Includes **≥15 acres** (with woodlands/wastelands) tests OR long-farmed **owner residence** tests under statute cited | **5-acre home base** may **not** meet agricultural acreage test—**assessor**; **120-acre** parcel is where test more likely |

## Operational recommendations

- Maintain a **one-page parcel table**: legal description, acreage, current use, prior classification, **March 15** owner, contact for **assessor** office.
- Pair greenbelt planning with **parcel intelligence** worksheets: [`Parcel intelligence package`](../topics/parcel-intelligence-package-east-tennessee-two-site.md).

## Professional / county gates

- **County assessor** interprets **productivity** and **portion in agricultural use** per statute language in capture.
- **CPA** for interplay with **income tax** basis and **rollback** exposure if use changes (capture discusses rollback appeals at SBOE).

## What the wiki does **not** do

- Predict **assessed value** outcomes or **rollback** amounts—requires **county** evidence.
