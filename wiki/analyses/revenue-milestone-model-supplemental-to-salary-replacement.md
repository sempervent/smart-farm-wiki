# Revenue milestone model — supplemental income to salary replacement

## Purpose

Translate the business plan’s **economic arc** into **fillable milestones**: **supplemental** cash first, then **meaningful** operating contribution, then a **salary-replacement band**—without inventing **your** household budget. Pairs with [`East Tennessee two-site farm business plan — revenue model and milestones`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-revenue-and-phased-income.md).

**Financial layer links**: [`Enterprise unit economics — worksheet methodology`](enterprise-unit-economics-worksheet-methodology-two-site-smart-farm.md), [`Farm accounting baseline`](farm-accounting-baseline-chart-of-accounts-enterprise-pl-two-site-smart-farm.md).

---

## Required inputs

| Input | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `HH_NEED` | Annual **household** cash need **from all sources** — **placeholder** until family finance work |
| `FARM_SHARE_TARGET` | Farm share of `HH_NEED` for **replacement narrative** — **%** band |
| `OFFFARM` | Off-farm wages + **benefits imputation** if quitting is in scope |
| **Books / management P&L** | Same categories as accounting baseline—**not** a different story than taxes without documentation |
| **Split-site and automation costs** | Ensure **telemetry OPEX** and **extra miles** do not vanish from **CONTRIB_MARGIN** |
| **Scenario ID** | **S1/S2** enterprise mix—**milestones may differ** by scenario |
| **Family labor shadow rate** | For **M3**—**$/hr** band, not a single guess |

---

## Outputs supported

| Output | Decision |
|--------|----------|
| **M0–M4** pass/fail | **When** to claim supplemental vs replacement |
| **Bridge years** | Explicit **years** where farm **subsidizes** learning—**not** hidden in optimism |
| **Scale freeze** | Tie to **negative** trailing margin—[`remediation backlog`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-remediation-backlog.md) |
| **Evidence list** | What **document** proves each milestone (bank, P&L, tax, sale bills) |

---

## Assumptions

| Assumption | Note |
|------------|------|
| **Milestones are scenario-aware** | If **S2** replaces **S1**, **restate** targets—do not silently carry **M4**. |
| **Low-labor priority** | A milestone can be **met** on **revenue** but **fail** on **labor**—track **both** if policy requires. |
| **Automation** | **Net** family hours matter: **field** minus **triage**—[`Instrumentation ROI model`](instrumentation-roi-model-two-site-smart-farm.md). |
| **Two sites** | **Revenue** may clear at **buyer dock** while **margin** dies in **haul**—include **all-in** **channel** cost in **evidence**. |

## Milestone ladder (placeholders)

| ID | Milestone | Metric | Target | Evidence | Gate / phase |
|----|-----------|--------|--------|----------|--------------|
| M0 | **Baseline** | 12-mo household + farm **ledger** exists | **Y/N** | Spreadsheet / software export | Phase 0 |
| M1 | **Supplemental** | Farm cash to household (definition: `___`) | **`$___/yr`** or **%** of `HH_NEED` | Bank + categorization | Phase 1–2 |
| M2 | **Positive operating** | `CONTRIB_MARGIN` **>** 0 for year | **Y** in year **___** | Management P&L | Phase 2 |
| M3 | **Material supplement** | Farm net after family labor charge at **`$___–$___/hr`** | **`$___/yr`** | Worksheet + labor log | Phase 3 |
| M4 | **Salary band** | Farm cash ≥ `FARM_SHARE_TARGET` × `HH_NEED` | **`$___/yr`** for **N** **years** | Bank + books | Phase 4 |

## Branching logic

| If… | Then… |
|-----|--------|
| **M2** missed **2** years | **Freeze** scale; cut herd or raise lease share per [`Recommended enterprise strategy`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-recommended-enterprise-strategy.md) |
| **M4** met but off-farm needed for **health insurance** | Do **not** claim full replacement—**adjust** milestone |
| **Enterprise mix changes** | **Re-baseline** **M1–M4** targets and **evidence** set |
| **Trailing margin negative** | **Scale freeze**—[`remediation backlog` §13](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-remediation-backlog.md) |
