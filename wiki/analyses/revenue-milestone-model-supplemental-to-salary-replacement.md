# Revenue milestone model — supplemental income to salary replacement

## Purpose

Translate the business plan’s **economic arc** into **fillable milestones**: **supplemental** **cash** **first**, then **meaningful** **operating** **margin**, then **salary-replacement** **band**—without **inventing** **your** **household** **budget**. Pairs with [`East Tennessee two-site farm business plan — revenue model and milestones`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-revenue-and-phased-income.md).

## Key decisions supported

| Decision | Milestone use |
|----------|----------------|
| **When** to **quit** or **reduce** off-farm work | **Only** after **milestone** rows **met** **with** **evidence** |
| **Herd / scale** increase | **After** **contribution** **margin** **gate** |
| **Second revenue stream** | **After** **labor** + **margin** **gates** |

## Definitions (fill)

| Term | Definition / formula |
|------|----------------------|
| `HH_NEED` | Annual **household** **cash** need **from all sources** — **`$___`** |
| `FARM_SHARE_TARGET` | **Farm** **share** **of** `HH_NEED` **for** **salary replacement** — **`___%`** |
| `OFFFARM` | **Off-farm** **wages** **+** **benefits** **value** — **`$___/yr`** |
| `CONTRIB_MARGIN` | **Revenue** − **variable** **costs** (incl. **paid** **inputs**, **vet**, **fuel**) — **method** **in** **books** |

## Milestone ladder (placeholders)

| ID | Milestone | Metric | Target | Evidence | Gate |
|----|-----------|--------|--------|----------|------|
| M0 | **Baseline** | **12-mo** **household** + **farm** **ledger** exists | **Y/N** | **Spreadsheet** | Phase 0 |
| M1 | **Supplemental** | **Farm** **cash** **+** **accrual** **adj.** | **`$___/yr`** **or** **%** **of** `HH_NEED` | **Tax** **/** **books** | Phase 1–2 |
| M2 | **Positive operating** | `CONTRIB_MARGIN` **>** **0** **for** **year** | **Y** **in** **year** **___** | **P&L** | Phase 2 |
| M3 | **Material** **supplement** | **Farm** **net** **after** **family** **labor** **charge** **`$___/hr`** | **`$___/yr`** | **Worksheet** | Phase 3 |
| M4 | **Salary band** | **Farm** **cash** **≥** `FARM_SHARE_TARGET` **×** `HH_NEED` | **`$___/yr`** **for** **N** **years** | **Bank** **+** **books** | Phase 4 |

## Revenue stream mix (by phase — edit)

| Phase | Primary streams (plan default) | Share of farm cash (est %) |
|-------|--------------------------------|----------------------------|
| 0–1 | Lease / **none** / **costs** only | |
| 2 | Grazing sales ± lease | |
| 3 | + scale or **second** stream | |
| 4 | + **direct** **or** **value-add** **optional** | |

## Branching logic

| If… | Then… |
|-----|--------|
| **M2** **missed** **2** **years** | **Freeze** **scale**; **cut** **herd** **or** **raise** **lease** **share** per [`Recommended enterprise strategy`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-recommended-enterprise-strategy.md) |
| **M4** **met** **but** **off-farm** **needed** **for** **health** **insurance** | **Do not** **claim** **full** **replacement**—**adjust** **milestone** |
