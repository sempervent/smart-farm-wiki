# Executive summary

**Hub**: [`Planning framework`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-framework.md)

## What this is

A **wiki-native**, **phased** business plan for an **East Tennessee** **two-site** operation: **~5 acres** = **home + control center** (smart house, workshop/lab, telemetry spine); **~120 acres** = **primary production**, **~35 minutes** away. **Objective order**: **profit** → **low labor** → **resilience**. **Horizon**: **10 years** from side income toward **possible** **salary replacement**—**not** **guaranteed**.

## Strategic choice (strategy family)

**Phased mixed** **enterprise** with **grazing-led** **beef** **as** **primary** **120-acre** **engine**, **optional** **lease/contract** **stabilizers**, and **deferred** **high-labor** **or** **long-payback** **enterprises** **until** **validated**. **Rationale** **and** **branching** **logic**: [`Recommended enterprise strategy`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-recommended-enterprise-strategy.md). **Full** **scenario** **matrix**: [`Enterprise options analysis`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-enterprise-options-analysis.md).

## Operating model (one paragraph)

**Batch** **field** **work** **at** **120** **around** **a** **weekly** **rhythm**; **use** **homestead** **for** **planning**, **records**, **broker**, **spares**, **and** **development**. **Telemetry** **targets** **water**, **power**, **and** **gates** **first** **to** **reduce** **panic** **trips**—**not** **cameras** **everywhere** **on** **day** **one**.

## Phases (snapshot)

| Phase | Essence |
|-------|---------|
| **0** | **No-regret** **groundwork**, **stop** **rules**, **access** **clarity** |
| **1** | **Land** **intelligence**, **market** **probe**, **pilot** **sensor** |
| **2** | **First** **real** **grazing** **business** **+** **records** |
| **3** | **Scale** **or** **second** **stream** **under** **labor** **cap** |
| **4** | **Salary-replacement** **architecture** **if** **metrics** **hold** |

## Economics (qualitative)

- **Short-term**: **supplemental** **income** **(lease**, **small** **sales** **)** **while** **off-farm** **cash** **dominates**.
- **Long-term**: **operating** **margin** **+** **disciplined** **scale** **toward** **household** **cash** **target** **(fill** **number** **in** **revenue** **page** **)**.

## Top risks (see full register)

1. **Price** **/** **weather** **margin** **squeeze**  
2. **Debt** **vs** **thin** **margin**  
3. **Labor** **surge** **without** **coverage**  
4. **Telemetry** **false** **confidence** **/** **alert** **fatigue**  
5. **Liability** **/** **zoning** **surprises**  

## What must be true before heavy CAPEX

**G1** **gate**: **land** **+** **water** **+** **legal** **use** **clarified**; **no** **full** **herd** **before** **[`validation backlog`](east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan-validation-backlog.md)** **minimums**.
