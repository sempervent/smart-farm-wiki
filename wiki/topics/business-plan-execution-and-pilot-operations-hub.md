**Purpose**: **Router** for **Phase 0–1 execution**, **pilots**, **validation (V\*)**, and **gates (G\*)**—distinct from the **strategic narrative** chapters in the business plan package. Use this when you are **doing** the work, not **reading** the thesis.

**What this hub owns**: **Where to start** for execution artifacts and **how** they relate to the **package hub**. It does **not** duplicate the full chapter TOC—that stays on [`East Tennessee two-site farm business plan (package)`](../business-plan/east-tennessee-two-site-farm-business-plan.md).

**What it does not own**: **Financial** thresholds as live numbers—those are worksheets and family evidence; the wiki holds **structure** and **gates**, not your bank account.
