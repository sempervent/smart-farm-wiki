# Tennessee farm business compliance package

**Purpose**: **Single router** for the **legal / tax / insurance / labor shell** around the **East Tennessee two-site** plan (**Claxton** `SITE_HOME` in **Anderson County**; **Demory** `SITE_FARM` in **Campbell County**). This package **surfaces official and extension-primary sources** already in `raw/` and adds **2026-04-22** IRS/DOL/SOS URL captures—**not** a substitute for **attorneys, CPAs, insurance agents, or county officials**.

**Epistemic bar**: Each child page separates **(A) official requirement**, **(B) operational recommendation**, **(C) professional-review gate**, **(D) Tennessee vs federal**, and **(E) two-site nuance** (which parcel triggers which filing).
