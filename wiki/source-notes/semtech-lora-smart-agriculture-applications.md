**Summary**

Semtech marketing summary tying LoRa/LoRaWAN to smart agriculture: population growth framing, long-range low-power sensor meshes, example use cases (weather, irrigation, livestock, greenhouses, soil, water), and mention of drone-mounted gateways extending coverage. Includes an example claim of large water savings on commercial farms with LoRa-based systems—treat as vendor illustration unless corroborated.

**Claims worth carrying forward**

- LoRa fits unlicensed-spectrum, multi-year battery sensor deployments common in rural operations.
- Drone-borne gateways appear in research as a way to reach sparse terrestrial sensors.
