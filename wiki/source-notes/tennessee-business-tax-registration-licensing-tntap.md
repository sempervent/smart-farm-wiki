**Key claims** (cite raw; re-verify on Revenue site):

- In-state locations with gross receipts **> $3,000** but **< $100,000** (thresholds per **2023** law for TY end on/after **12/31/2023**) require **minimal activity license** from county/municipal clerk; **≥ $100,000** requires **standard** license (**raw** excerpt).
- **Business tax** due **15th day of 4th month** after fiscal year end (excerpt).
- Up to **10 business days** for Revenue to register after application; **$15** license registration fee to county/municipal clerk per new business (excerpt).
