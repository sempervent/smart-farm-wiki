# Low-volume roads BMPs — Chapter 7 roadway drainage (PDF)

**Raw PDF**: [`raw/processed/2026/ch7.pdf`](../../raw/processed/2026/ch7.pdf) (chapter titled **Drainage of Low-Volume Roads** in BMP manual)  
**Machine extract**: [`raw/processed/2026/ch7-extracted.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/ch7-extracted.md)

**Summary**: Emphasizes **roadway surface drainage** as a primary water-quality and cost control; **“drainage, drainage, drainage”**. Design surfaces to disperse flow using **rolling grades** and **outsloped, insloped, or crowned** alignments; **cross-drains** and **rolling / broad-based dips** to interrupt flow paths before erosion accelerates; discusses limits on **steep grades** and need for **ditch and cross-drain systems** when grades exceed thresholds (see extract for narrative detail).
