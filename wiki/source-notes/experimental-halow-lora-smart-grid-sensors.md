# Source note: Experimental field comparison of Wi‑Fi HaLow and LoRa (smart grid)

**Raw path:** [`raw/processed/2026/experimental-field-comparison-wi-fi-halow-and-lora-smart-grid.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/experimental-field-comparison-wi-fi-halow-and-lora-smart-grid.md)

**Summary**

Peer-reviewed **Sensors (MDPI, 2023)** article (PMCID PMC10490590) reporting **field experiments** comparing **Wi‑Fi HaLow** (802.11ah) against **Wi‑Fi n** and **LoRa** for throughput, latency, and reliability, with secure remote-evaluation architectures. Positions HaLow as promising for smart-grid-style IoT requirements; explicitly scopes out LoRaWAN MAC and power measurements.

**Claims worth carrying forward**

- Early HaLow performance literature was often simulation-limited; this work emphasizes **authentic** outdoor/lab-distance testing with then-available hardware.
- Comparison is **LoRa PHY link** (not full LoRaWAN network server stack) vs HaLow.
