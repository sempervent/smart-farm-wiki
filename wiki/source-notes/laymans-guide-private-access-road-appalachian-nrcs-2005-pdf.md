# Layman's guide to private access road construction — Southern Appalachians (PDF)

**Summary**: **Second edition 2005**, NRCS / Forest Service contributions—mountain **access road** planning, **stream sedimentation** risk, and “**cheaper to build right once**” framing. Includes tables on **maximum sustained grades** (e.g. **under 10%** for gravel, **under 6%** for natural soil/grass surface per extract), **avoid steep grades at curves**, **waterbars / dips** spacing vs grade, and erosion control integrated through construction.
