**Raw path:** [`raw/processed/2026/lorawan-can-save-farmers-money-and-increase-yields.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/lorawan-can-save-farmers-money-and-increase-yields.md)

**Summary**

Vendor-oriented overview of LoRaWAN in smart agriculture: LPWAN traits, gateway + cloud end-to-end framing, precision-ag benefits (irrigation, soil metrics, livestock), operational challenges (protocol diversity, reliability in weather), and TEKTELIC case studies (e.g. KIWI soil sensor, cider apple and hops projects, poultry environmental monitoring with KONA Macro gateway). Marketing tone; treat figures (e.g. range, battery life) as claims to verify against independent sources.

**Claims worth carrying forward**

- LoRaWAN positions as lower infrastructure cost than cellular for many farm sensor backhauls.
- Soil moisture, microclimate, and livestock telemetry are recurring precision-ag motifs.
