**Raw path:** [`raw/processed/2026/bps-gps-alternative-nobody-heard-of.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/bps-gps-alternative-nobody-heard-of.md)

**Summary**

NAB/trade-show blog notes on **Broadcast Positioning System (BPS)** tied to **ATSC 3.0 / NEXTGEN TV**, comparing **TV-broadcast** timing signals to **GPS PPS** on a scope (demo claims ~**10 ns** alignment in the write-up). Positions BPS as a potential **terrestrial backup** to GNSS for timing-critical infrastructure; includes consumer **PPS/TGPIO** motherboard teaser.
