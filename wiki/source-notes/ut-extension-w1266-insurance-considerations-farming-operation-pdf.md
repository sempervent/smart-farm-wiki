# UT Extension W1266 — insurance considerations for your farming operation (PDF)

**Primary files**: [`w1266.pdf`](../../raw/processed/2026/w1266.pdf) · [`w1266-extracted.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/w1266-extracted.md)

**Authority**: **UT Extension** + **Farm Bureau of Tennessee** specialist co-authorship (see PDF title page in extract)—**extension/education**, not an insurance policy or guarantee of coverage.

## Evidence summary

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Abstract** | Short extension bulletin framing **five risk categories**, **agent selection**, and **coverage families** (liability, physical damage, product liability) with agritourism called out as often needing **commercial** liability vs general farm liability. |
| **Authority mix** | **Land-grant extension** publication (**high** for *education*; **not** a contract of insurance). |
| **Decision relevance** | Question list for **insurance agents**; distinguishes **general farm liability** vs **commercial business liability** for non-typical-farm activities; emphasizes **reading the policy** and **matching carrier appetite** for agriculture. |
| **Canonical wiki links** | [`Farm insurance and liability checklist — two-site Tennessee`](../analyses/farm-insurance-liability-checklist-two-site-tennessee.md) · [`Tennessee farm business compliance package`](../topics/tennessee-farm-business-compliance-package.md) |

**Key claims** (from extract; public-safe):

- Liability products should match **acreage owned/rented** and **enterprise type** (production vs **agritourism**).
- **Product liability** may matter for **direct sales** / on-farm consumption contexts; some buyers may **require** proof of coverage.

**Open questions / follow-ups**

- Pair with **carrier-specific** endorsements for **on-farm processing**, **events**, and **off-farm** product delivery when those phases activate.
