**Key claims** (public-safe):

- **Tennessee** (captured **TDEC** page + **2018** program PDF) describes a **wild ginseng harvest season** on **private land with landowner permission** running **September 1–December 31** (inclusive), with **plants** having **three or more prongs** and **mature red berries**, and **berries replanted** after harvest; **dealer** permitting applies to **purchase for resale/export** (see captures for **buying-season** dates and **fees**).
- **Commercial stratified seed** should stay **refrigerated** until planting; **MTSU-region** guide recommends **July–August** sourcing windows (confirm with your vendor).

**Open questions / follow-ups**
