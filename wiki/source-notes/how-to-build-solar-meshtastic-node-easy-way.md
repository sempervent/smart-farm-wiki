# Source note: How to build a solar Meshtastic node the easy way (ThinkNode M6)

**Raw path:** [`raw/processed/2026/how-to-build-a-solar-meshtastic-node-the-easy-way.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/how-to-build-a-solar-meshtastic-node-the-easy-way.md)

**Summary**

Product-focused guide to deploying a pre-integrated outdoor solar Meshtastic node (Elecrow ThinkNode M6): IP65 enclosure, 6 W panel with MPPT, ~7000 mAh dual 18650, nRF52840 + Semtech SX1262, L76K GPS, Meshtastic pre-installed, BLE setup via mobile app, mounting and antenna assembly, and expansion via SP11 UART/I²C for environmental or agriculture sensors.

**Claims worth carrying forward**

- All-in-one solar nodes reduce build time and weatherproofing risk versus fully DIY assembly.
- Clear sky placement matters for both PV yield and LoRa line-of-sight.
