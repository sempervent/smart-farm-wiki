# Kentucky EPSC — protecting slopes to prevent gullies (PDF)

**Raw PDF**: [`raw/processed/2026/epsc-protect-slopes.pdf`](../../raw/processed/2026/epsc-protect-slopes.pdf)  
**Machine extract**: [`raw/processed/2026/epsc-protect-slopes-extracted.md`](../../raw/processed/2026/epsc-protect-slopes-extracted.md)

**Summary**: Construction-era **slope protection**—**seed + mulch** as default; **blankets/mats** on many slopes **steeper than 3:1**; **divert upland runoff**; break long slopes; **fiber rolls on contour** every **75–125 ft** on long slopes; table of **slope steepness** vs ratio/degrees; notes **track up/down** hill on treads for stability vs other track patterns affecting erosion sensitivity.
