Wi-Fi HaLow® (pronounced HEYlow) is the first Wi-Fi standard tailored to meet the Internet of Things (IoT) needs. It’s an open standard wireless network technology operating in the 850-950 MHz range. By operating in the sub-GHz range, this ultra-low-power wireless protocol can connect more IoT devices at much longer distances and much lower power than traditional Wi-Fi. The protocol is ratified by the IEEE 802.11ah task group and dubbed Wi-Fi HaLow® by the Wi-Fi Alliance.  
Morse Micro and our trusted module partners offer commercially available Wi-Fi HaLow® modules that are globally certified and ready to deploy. These modules are designed to power the next generation of IoT solutions, ensuring rapid integration into any IoT application across various industries.

A range of Wi-Fi HaLow® modules, based on our SoCs and developed in partnership with leading technology providers, offer an exceptional range, quality, interoperability, and scalability for IoT applications. With extended connectivity, ultra-reliable performance, and the capacity to support thousands of devices connected to a single access point, these modules deliver robust solutions for even the most demanding environments. Designed to integrate seamlessly into your projects, they combine top-tier engineering with the power of Wi-Fi HaLow®, ensuring efficiency and scale at any level.
