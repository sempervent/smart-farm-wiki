## SENSORS

600 mm probe length, 6 soil moisture, temperature and salinity sensors. Other lengths, number of sensors: contact us  
  
SOIL MOISTURE  
Range: 0... 100 % volumetric water content (VWC)  
Resolution: 0.01 %  
Accuracy: ±0.03 %

SOIL TEMPERATURE  
Range: -20... 60 °C  
Accuracy: ±2.0 °C at 25 °C  
Note: Inaccuracy can be higher under moderate-to-high solar radiation.  
Resolution: ±0.01°C

SALINITY  
Range: 0... 8000 volumetric ion content (VIC)  
Accuracy: Not specified
