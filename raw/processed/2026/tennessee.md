### Agriculture Drives Tennessee’s Economy

- Tennessee crops contribute $5.2 billion to the state’s economy.
- Top crops insured include soybeans, corn, and cotton.

### Crop Insurance Is Vital To Tennessee’s Agricultural Sector

*“Without crop insurance as a risk management tool, a disaster as we had in 2012 would not be survivable to many family operations.”* – Trent Patterson, Lake City, Tennessee

- In 2025, crop insurance protected 2.9 million acres providing $1.4 billion in liability protection.
- Farmers paid $42.6 million for this insurance coverage.
- Crop insurers paid $150 million to cover crop losses in 2025.

  
The private Crop-Hail insurance product provided an additional $89.8 million in liability to protect Tennessee crops in 2025.
