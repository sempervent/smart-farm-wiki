## SENSORS

VOLUMETRIC WATER CONTENT (VWC)  
Range:  
Mineral soil 0.00 … 0.70 m³/m³  
Soilless media 0.0 … 1.0 m³/m³  
Apparent dielectric permittivity εa: 1 (air) to 80 (water)  
Resolution: 0.001 m³/m³  
Accuracy: ±0.03 m³/m³ (±3% VWC) typical in mineral soils

TEMPERATURE  
Range: -40... +60 °C  
Resolution: 0.1 °C  
Accuracy: ±0.5 °C from −40 … 0 °C, ±0.3 °C from 0 … +60 °C

BULK ELECTRICAL CONDUCTIVITY  
Range: 0 … 20 dS/m (bulk)  
Resolution: 0.001 dS/m  
Accuracy: ±(5% +0.01 dS/m) from 0 … 10 dS/m, ±8% from 10 … 20 dS/m
