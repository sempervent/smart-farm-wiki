## Examples of Farm Loans

[**![Laurie and Brian Olson](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/sites/default/files/documents/laurie-and-brian-olson.jpg "Laurie and Brian Olson")**](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/farm-operating-loans "Farm Operating Loans")

### Operating Loans

[Operating Loans](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/farm-operating-loans "Farm Operating Loans") can be used to purchase livestock, seed and equipment. It can also cover farm operating costs and family living expenses while a farm gets up and running. [Read more.](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/farm-operating-loans "Farm Operating Loans")

Meet a Farmer: An Operating Loan helped Alaska farmers Brian and Laurie Olson expand their berry operation. [Read more](https://www.farmers.gov/connect/blog/loans-and-grants/savoring-fruits-labor "Savoring the Fruits of Labor | Farmers.gov").

---

[**![Jesse Paul Checking the Syrup sugar content](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/sites/default/files/documents/jesse-paul.jpg "Jesse Paul")**](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/farm-ownership-loans "Farm Ownership Loans")

### Farm Ownership Loans

[Farm Ownership Loans](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/farm-ownership-loans "Farm Ownership Loans") can be used to purchase or expand a farm or ranch. This loan can help with paying closing costs, constructing or improving buildings on the farm, or to help conserve and protect soil and water resources. [Read more](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/farm-ownership-loans "Farm Ownership Loans").

Meet a Farmer: Jesse and Tracey Paul purchased a farm using a Farm Ownership Loan. Today their family produces pure maple syrup in Trout Creek, Michigan. [Read more](https://www.farmers.gov/connect/blog/loans-and-grants/farming-sweet-midwest "Farming Sweet in the Midwest | Farmers.gov").

---

[**![Nik Bouman's New Equipment](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/sites/default/files/documents/nik-bouman-city-farms.jpg "Nik Bouman's New Equipment")**](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/microloans "Microloan Programs")

### Microloans

[Microloans](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/microloans "Microloan Programs") are a type of Operating or Farm Ownership Loan. They’re designed to meet the needs of small and beginning farmers, or for non-traditional and specialty operations by easing some of the requirements and offering less paperwork. [Read more](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/microloans "Microloan Programs").

Meet a Farmer: With the help of a low-interest microloan, Nik Bouman was able to purchase new equipment for his hydroponic basil farm, allowing him to quadruple his business and expand access to fresh basil in his community. [Read more](https://www.farmers.gov/connect/blog/farm-life/keeping-it-fresh-last-frontier "Keeping it Fresh in the Last Frontier | Farmers.gov").

[**![Payton Farmer and Macy](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/sites/default/files/documents/payton-farmer.jpg "Payton Farmer and Macy")**](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/youth-loans "Youth Loans")

### Youth Loans

[Youth Loans](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/youth-loans "Youth Loans") are a type of Operating Loan for young people between 10-20 years old who need assistance with an educational agricultural project. Typically, these youth are participating in 4-H clubs, FFA, or a similar organization. [Read more](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/youth-loans "Youth Loans").

Meet a Farmer: Payton Farmer received a youth loan to purchase a breeding heifer and began building her herd. [Read more](https://www.farmers.gov/connect/blog/loans-and-grants/show-stopper "The Show Stopper | Farmers.gov").

### Loan Programs for Native American Tribes

[Loan Programs for](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/native-american-loans) [Native American Trib](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/native-american-loans "Native American Tribal Loans") [e](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/native-american-loans) [s](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/native-american-loans "Native American Tribal Loans") help Tribes acquire land interests within a tribal reservation or Alaskan native community; advance current farming operations; provide financial prospects for Native American communities; increase agricultural productivity; and save cultural farmland for future generations. [Read more](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/native-american-loans "Native American Tribal Loans")

### Emergency Loans

[Emergency Loans](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/emergency-farm-loans "Emergency Farm Loans") help farmers and ranchers recover from production and physical losses due to drought, flooding, other natural disasters or losses. [Read more](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/emergency-farm-loans "Emergency Farm Loans").

Meet a Farmer: Bruce and Jennifer McCall take pride in raising their sons on the farm and have used FSA programs and loans to expand their operation and recover from natural disasters. [Read more](http://fsa.blogs.govdelivery.com/2016/09/20/the-mccalls-keep-ag-all-in-the-family/ "The McCalls Keep Ag ‘All in the Family’ ").

## Direct Loans vs. Guaranteed Loans

![](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/sites/default/files/documents/davis.jpg "Davis") Many FSA loans are available as either Guaranteed Loans or Direct Loans.

Direct Loans are made directly from FSA to the farmer.

[**Guaranteed Loans**](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/guaranteed-farm-loans "Guaranteed Farm Loans") are made by a USDA-approved traditional lender with the backing of FSA. [Read more about Guaranteed Loans and access the lender toolkit here](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/programs-and-services/farm-loan-programs/guaranteed-farm-loans "Guaranteed Farm Loans").
